About This Website

This website presents testimonials from protesters injured by the SPD during the summer of 2020. All materials are a matter of public record in the court system.

We worked with legal organizations including:

  • American Civil Liberties Union
  • Stritmatter Kessler Koehler & Moore
  • Cedar Law Group

This website was created by volunteers associated with the University of Washington School of Public Health.
